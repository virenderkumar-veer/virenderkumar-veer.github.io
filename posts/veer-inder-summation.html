📘 Veer Inder Summation Formula

Contributor: Virender Kumar (Veer Inder)

This formula was designed as a simplified approach to compute summations and visualize patterns in sequences.

🔢 Formula:

∑(i = 1 to n) = n(n + 1) / 2   ← Standard Formula
∑(i = 1 to n) = (n × (next number)) ÷ 2   ← Veer Inder Interpretation

It’s useful in foundational mathematics and in making concepts easier for students and educators.
